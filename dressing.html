Greek Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
  • 3 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt

    • Honey Musturd Dressing

    Ingredients:

    • 1/4 cup dijon mustard
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
    • 3-4 Tbsp. honey
    • 1 lemon, freshly squeezed
    • 1/2 tsp. salt
    • Dash of pepper

    Tangerine Dressing

    Ingredients:

    • 1/2 cup good quality extra virgin olive oil
    • 1/4 cup fresh tangerine juice
    • 2 teaspoon honey
    • zest and juice of 1 small lemon
    • pinch sea salt
    • freshly ground pepper
